Why recruitment operations break

The patterns
are always
the same.

What worked at 5 people breaks at 20. Hiring more doesn't fix it.

01

Your team is doing work a system should handle

Updating ATS/CRM manually. Screening and chasing candidates. Sending the same email again. Every hour spent on this is billable time burned.

02

You're paying for tools, and for people to babysit them

You pay for software. Then you pay people to babysit it. That's overhead.

03

You're hiring to compensate for broken processes

A new hire doesn't fix a broken process. They inherit it. Headcount grows. Output doesn't.

04

You don't have real visibility

Pipeline data in five places. Decisions on gut feel. By the time you see what went wrong, the placement is lost.

What I typically see within the first 30–90 days
  • → 10–20+ hours/week removed from manual work
  • → Fewer tools. Clear workflows. Faster placements.
  • → Founder out of day-to-day execution.

What I typically find in almost every growing business

20–50%

of recruiter time consumed by manual ATS/CRM updates, status chasing, and admin between systems. Not in front of candidates or clients.

3–5

tools across job intake, screening, submissions, and client comms. None of them connected to each other.

No standard process

for job intake, candidate submission, or client handoff that runs the same way twice. Every recruiter improvises.

One person

holds the business together because they're the only one who knows how everything is really working. Usually the founder. That's the risk.

Example engagement

What this looks like
in practice.

18-person recruitment agency · €2M+ revenue · growing fast.

Before
  • Recruiters spent 2+ hours/day on manual ATS updates after every call
  • No standard submission process. Every recruiter sent CVs differently.
  • Client feedback and follow-ups managed in personal email. No team visibility.
  • Founder approving every placement because no one knew who owned the decision
After
  • ATS updates automated via email integrations. Zero manual data entry.
  • One standardised submission workflow across the whole team
  • Client communication centralised. Full pipeline visibility for the founder.
  • Ownership defined at every stage: sourcing, submission, interview, placement
8+ hours saved per recruiter per week.
Founder back to business development. Not stuck in delivery approvals.
